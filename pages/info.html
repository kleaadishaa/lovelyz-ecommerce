At Glow & Radiance Skincare, we strive to provide the best shopping experience for our customers. Our transport and payment policies are designed to make the process smooth, secure, and transparent. Below are the details:

1. Shipping Information

We offer reliable and timely shipping to ensure you receive your skincare products as quickly as possible. Shipping times may vary depending on the destination, and we work closely with our trusted couriers to keep you informed about any potential delays.

  • Order Processing Time: Orders are processed within 1–2 business days (Monday to Friday, excluding holidays).
  • Shipping Times:
    • Domestic Shipping: 3–5 business days
    • International Shipping: 7–14 business days
  • Shipping Costs: The cost of shipping is calculated during checkout based on your delivery address. We also offer free shipping for orders over $50 (domestic only).
  • Shipping Methods: We use a range of trusted carriers such as FedEx, UPS, and DHL to deliver your products efficiently and securely.

2. Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, we will send you a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to check the status of your shipment.

3. Packaging

We take great care in packaging all products to ensure they arrive safely. All products are securely packed in high-quality, eco-friendly materials to minimize damage during transportation.

4. Payment Methods

We offer a variety of secure payment options for your convenience. You can choose the method that best suits you during the checkout process.

  • Credit & Debit Cards: We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover.
  • PayPal: Safe and secure transactions via your PayPal account.
  • Apple Pay/Google Pay: Quick and easy payments using your preferred wallet.
  • Gift Cards: We offer Gift Cards for purchase that can be used during checkout.

All payments are processed using secure, encrypted gateways, ensuring your personal and financial information is protected.

5. Taxes

Sales tax is applied to orders depending on your delivery location. The final tax amount will be displayed during the checkout process.

6. Cancellations & Returns

Order Cancellations: You can cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, it will be processed, and you’ll need to follow our returns policy for any changes.

Returns and Refunds: We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not, we offer a 30-day return policy on unused and unopened products. Once the return is approved, we’ll process your refund.

  • Customers are responsible for return shipping fees unless the item is defective or damaged.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method, and processing can take 3–5 business days.

7. Customer Support

Our customer service team is available to help with any issues related to transport and payment. If you need assistance, please contact us at support@glowandradiance.com, and we’ll ensure that your issue is resolved promptly.

This Transport and Payment Policy ensures that we maintain transparency and that you receive your skincare products efficiently and securely. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to reach out to our customer service team.
